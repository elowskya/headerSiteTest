Things to Be Aware of

There are different times of the year when parks offer transportation to trail heads, fees vary, weather can be very hot or cold

Check out local maps and research trails for closures

Parks can have extreme temperatures, for instance Zion can be over 100 degrees and Mount Ranier can be below freezing

  • Know when to go
  • Go with a plan
